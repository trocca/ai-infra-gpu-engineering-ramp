# Progress Tracker

One row per week. Fill in every Friday after the self-check.

- **Topics done**: did you cover the week's plan? (✅ / partial / ❌ + what slipped)
- **Labs done**: which labs/exercises you actually ran
- **Mock/self-check score**: Friday self-check %, or mock exam % in review weeks
- **Confidence (1–5)**: 1 = couldn't pass this domain today, 5 = exam-ready

## Red flags to act on

- Any week with confidence ≤ 2 → repeat that week's self-check before moving on; steal
  time from a stronger domain.
- Mock < 80 % → postpone-or-cram decision: identify the two weakest domains from the
  answer key and drill only those in the remaining days.
- Two consecutive weeks with "topics done: partial" → the plan is overloaded for your
  schedule; cut optional resources, keep exit criteria.
